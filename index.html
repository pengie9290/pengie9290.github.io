This was a little project I made recently for my web design class.

This "database" is filled with salvaged files, belonging to Daedalus Labs, the "big bad" organization of the world I've been writing for the last few years.

(To be clear, unlike the documents themselves, none of this light-grey text is canon.)

Report Author: Dr. Ethan Thorne

Location ID: #X-L-4753

Common Name: The Barren Isle

Public Knowledge: The Barren Isle is an uninhabited island off the northeast shore of Gaela. Little to no life lives on the island or in the surrounding waters. Dead, dried out vegetation can be found all over the island. Reportedly, desiccated carcasses of wildlife could also be found near the shore before the Holy Order arrived, took hold of the empty Facility #27325 for their own purposes, and removed them. Those who have been on the island reportedly often find themselves incapable of using magic. The majority of the island is covered in a forest of dead trees. There are no known records or folktales of people entering this forest ever returning.

Examination Results:

All wireless communications cut out before reaching the island’s shore, and are impossible to reestablish without first moving a certain distance from the island. The use of drones to conduct unmanned surveys of the island is impossible. Just past the island’s shore, the same occurs for all electronic equipment. Though dead, the forest’s trees are too densely packed together to make out any significant details from afar. Seven parties have entered the forest to conduct examinations and collect samples. The party tasked with searching only a few meters past the forest’s perimeter is the only one to have returned. All employees who have set foot and remained on the island for longer than a day have reported feeling ill, but have not known how to describe their symptoms in detail. Each has undergone medical examinations following their return, and not one showed any sign of illness or injury.

Samples have been collected including animal carcasses found just within the forest’s perimeter, pieces of vegetation, and several soil, rock, and water samples collected from all over the island. Barring the stomachs of the animal carcasses, no signs of decomposition have been found. All microorganisms found within collected samples appear to be as dead as the macroorganisms also collected.Carbon dating of the samples reveals that the death of every sample occurred approximately 1000 years ago.

Conclusions:

Given the location of Facility #27325 on this island, what’s left of Facility #32387 must also be on this island, near the center in the depths of the forest. As Facility #32387 was the site at which Subject #E-D-3 was created, and by extension the origin point of The Surge, a number of conclusions can be drawn. The deaths of the organisms on this island was likely caused by the Surge itself. There appears to be some form of energy radiating from the center of this island which slowly kills organisms living on it. This explains why no decomposition has occurred, as no foreign microorganisms carried by the wind or water can survive long enough to decompose anything. This energy is likely also the cause of both our equipment malfunctions and the dysfunctionality of magic while on the island.

As our equipment cannot function on the island, we are incapable of positively identifying the nature of this energy, though it is undoubtedly magic in nature. However, a likely hypothesis has been formed. Given the unique nature of this energy, which cannot be found anywhere else in the world, the source of it must be one-of-a-kind. Most likely, this energy emanates directly from Subject #E-D-3 itself, hidden in the depths of the island’s impassable forest.
